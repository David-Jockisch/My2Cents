David's 2 Cents

Pros of PS5

  • Upgradable storage using off the shelf M.2 SSDs
  • Peripheral support (Type C headphones for PS5 also work with PC, Switch)
  • PlayStation voice chat is top tier.
  • Can play all PS4 titles, some patched to run better (See cons)
  • (More Friends on PlayStation!)

Cons of PS5

  • Has Discord support but not a built in Discord app.
  • Without a storage upgrade, the on board SSD fills up fast.
  • Can only play PS1-3 games that have been loaded on the PlayStation store to work.
  • Could be a con- New slim PS5 has 2 front USB C ports instead of a type A and C

Pros of Xbox Series X

  • Gamepass is affordable and has a lot of day one games.
  • Has built in Discord app to connect to servers from the console.
  • Can play any Xbox game from OG Xbox to current gen. Will automatically find the best version of this.

Cons of Xbox Series X

  • For 2022/23 Xbox exclusive lineup was lacking.
  • Upgradable storage is proprietary and dollar per GB is very high.
  • Recent games are releasing with Series X and Xbox One versions. Not sure how the Xbox One version loads on Series X
  • Peripheral requires specific hardware. Xbox headphone must be made for Xbox. Might not work on other platforms.

Thoughts on Xbox Series S

Don't wastes your money or time on this turd. The PS4 has more power.
